Our Services

Life Skills Training

Some of these sessions include Self-worth Workshops, Stress Management, Time Management, Problem Solving, Goal Setting Workshops, and Addiction Awareness Programmes.

Individual Therapy

This involves supporting the client/addict address unresolved issues before, during and after the substance use. Our counsellors provide a therapeutic environment that fosters the necessary nurturing and guidance to facilitate change.

Mediation Services

Addiction Awareness Seminars

Interventions

Our Intervention model is geared towards helping the individual acknowledge the negative consequences of their addiction and increasing the chances of them seeking treatment. This Intervention process is a powerful therapeutic tool if administered by a trained professional. We are amongst the few in Trinidad and Tobago who offer this service. The price of this service ranges from $700-$1500.

Addiction Family Therapy

Addiction has an impact on every member of a household not just the individual with the substance abuse problem. Unlike individual therapy, which addresses the thoughts, behaviours and attitudes of the person seeking treatment for drug or alcohol addiction, family therapy strives to treat a group or network of individuals who are affected by the addiction. We at Catalyst Initiative believe that addiction affects everyone and family therapy is an indispensable part of the treatment process.

Relapse Prevention

Relapse is never an accident; it is a gradual break of defences which manifests first in thoughts and then behavioural elements where the extreme could be using the substance again. We at the Catalyst Initiative facilitate a very comprehensive Relapse Prevention Programme which helps clients evaluate their thoughts, feelings and actions to refocus their goal of a clean and sober life.
